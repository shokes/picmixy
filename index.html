About

Welcome to Picmixy, your source for the best quality of picutres. We are committed to giving you the best pictures with focus on creativity and uniquness. Founded in 2021 by Rogers Davis, who is a professional photography based in Los Angeles. Rogers attended the University of the London where he received a Bachelor's degree in Animation. Since moving to Los Angeles, he has spent time working at Motion networks, FOX Animation, ShadowMachine, and Hot House Productions.
